Antigravity CLI supports enterprise access through Google Cloud Platform. During the onboarding flow, you connect a GCP project to your account, which enables enterprise-level capabilities for your organization.

<Note>
  Enterprise authentication requires a GCP project and enterprise access. Contact your Google account team if you need to enable enterprise access for your organization.
</Note>

## Connect a GCP project

Enterprise setup happens as part of the CLI onboarding flow — you don't need to configure it separately. When you first run Antigravity CLI with an enterprise account, onboarding prompts you to connect a GCP project.

Connecting a GCP project during onboarding enables enterprise access for your CLI sessions going forward.
